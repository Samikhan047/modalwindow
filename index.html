About Layering and z-index

The z-index property controls stacking order along the Z axis.

Typical issues arise due to new stacking contexts.

Modal windows often use fixed positioning and a high z-index.

The backdrop darkens the background, focusing attention on the dialog.

Modal content must always appear above the backdrop.
